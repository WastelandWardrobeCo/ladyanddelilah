Field Notes From the Road

Not all encounters are survived. Some are only recorded after the fire goes out.

She does not move fast.

She removes everything that is not the movement.

Five arrows make a person honest.

Anything more is an excuse.

The bow is discipline.

The spear is control. The knife is the last room in the house.

The wolf does not wait for command.

She reads the ending first.

Some men believe they are being hunted.

They are being measured.

C'mon, Lady.

The Shape of the Series

This Is Not a Hero's Road

Delilah does not arrive to save the world. Lady does not ask permission to enter it. Together, they walk into places where cruelty has become organized and leave those places with fewer rooms.

The Hunter

Precision over rage.

Delilah is not reckless. She is exact. Every step is a decision. Every weapon has a distance. Every mistake belongs to someone else.

The Wolf

Partner, not pet.

Lady does not follow. Lady understands. When she moves, the room changes shape around her.

The Rule

Five is enough.

Five arrows. No waste. No excuses. If the quiver empties, the story has gotten close enough to use the knife.
