Field Record

This is not a safe road.

Delilah does not survive by luck. Lady is not a pet. Together, they move through a broken world where mercy is rare, monsters wear familiar faces, and every hunt leaves evidence behind.

The Hunter

Delilah

Red-haired, controlled, and ruthlessly exact. She wastes no movement, no breath, and no arrow.

The Wolf

Lady

An oversized gray dire wolf, protective and intelligent. Not a mount. Not a mascot. Her equal.

The Rule

Five Arrows

Delilah carries exactly five. Count them before the hunt begins. Count them again after.
